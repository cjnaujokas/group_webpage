Our Mission Statement

Were a group of tech enthusiasts who love tackling tough problems and building cool things together. Our team is all about creativity, learning, and making a positive impact with our work. We believe in the power of diverse ideas, having fun while we code, and always pushing ourselves (and each other) to improve. Whether were brainstorming or debugging, we bring our best and help each other out.

Rules

  1. Kind and Respectful: We’re here to support each other. No idea is too wild to share, and every voice matters.
  2. Accountable: We stick to our tasks and let each other know if we hit a roadblock. If you’re stuck, don’t struggle alone—ask for help!
  3. Keep Communication Open: Regular check-ins and updates are a must. We keep each other in the loop and share feedback to stay on track.
  4. Wins Over Solo Wins: We’re here to build something great together. Sharing credit and celebrating each other's wins is part of our vibe.
  5. Learn Together: There’s always something new to discover. We share skills, tips, and tricks to keep everyone sharp.
  6. Go with the Flow: Tech (and life) can throw curveballs. We stay flexible and keep a positive attitude, ready to adapt when things change.
